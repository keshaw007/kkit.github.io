CHAIRPERSON'S Message

Greetings…….!

All are aware that KESHAW KUMAR INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY, Texas, USA (KKIT) has started functioning in the Year 1993 and technically, is still in the establishment stage.

However, the Institute is striving to be a fore-runner in cutting edge technology research and education. I am delighted to share that the current Batch of students has passed out from the Institute and 1000 students out of 1200 got placement through campus interview with highest package offered is 200 M usd per annum and average package of 120 usd per annum. 200 students have opted for higher studies.

Further, you would be happy to know that out of 141 registered students of the 2021 Batch, 240 students are already placed through campus interview with 199 M as maximum package and 10.43 M Lakhs as average package per annum.

On the Internship front, 83 students out of 150 of final year 2020-21 have been placed for one semester Internship with average stipend of Rs. 14,350/- in various companies like TRDDC, BARC, Persistent, NARL ISRO, AP, Clever Tap, Mumbai, The Solar Labs, Noida, Bosch Ltd.

The Institute promotes research activities for Faculty Members in the form of internationally reviewed SCI/Scopus Journal Publications, Patents, Research Projects, FDP, Workshops and Industry Collaborative Consultancy. In last four years of operations of the Institute, the Faculties of the Institute have published 114 Research Papers in various Publications.

I am delighted to inform all the stakeholders that a Patent Application for “Virtual Signal System” developed at the Institute has been filed with the Office of the Controller General of Patents, Design & Trade Marks, Mumbai under the relevant provisions of the Patent Act, 1970 and the Patent Rules, 2003.

The Institute is fortunate to have Faculties who are not only competent in teaching but are also instrumental in implementing Research Projects for Science & Engineering Research Board (SERB) and programmes under DST-NIMAT, AICTE-ATAL etc.

I would like to assure all the students that IIITN understands the fast pace of changes occurring in the field of Information Technology. To cope up with the speed of changes, we undertake the review of Academic Curriculum on regular basis through the Board of Studies consisting of experts from and IITs/NITs & Industry Experts including inputs from the students. Fine tuning of course curriculum, introduction of new courses, modifications of course contents is being done regularly in accordance with the recommendations / suggestions provided by Board of Studies and approved by the Senate.

I take this opportunity to express my gratitude to all the stakeholders including Ministry of Education, Government of India, Department of Higher Education, Government of Maharashtra, Tata Consultancy Services, Industry Partner and all the Students, Faculties and Employees who are wholeheartedly supporting in the pursuit to achieve Vision & Mission Objectives of the Institute.

Dr. K.K. Singh
